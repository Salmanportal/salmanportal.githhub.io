About

Welcome to my homepage! I am a 4th year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at West Virginia University , supervised Dr. Nasser Nasrabadi. I will be spending the summer of 2022 with Apple Inc as a Machine Learning Intern.

Interests

  • Machine Learning: Deep learning, Computer vision, VR/AR, Image quality assessment Adversarial learning, Object detection, 3D, Gesture control, Robotics etc.

Programming Skills

  • Platforms: PyTorch, TorchScript, Tensoflow, SLAM, Flask, PyCharm, MATLAB, Kinect SDK, and GIT.
  • Languages: Python, C#, Matlab, C/C++, SQL, Java, PHP
  • Hardware Handling: Kinect Sensor, Robotic Arm, Autonomous Robots

Project Expertise

  • Facial Synthesis: Deployed an image synthesis project that converts facial sketches to facial RGB images of different ethnic backgrounds.
  • Facial Attributes Applied various facial attributes (Brown, Blonde, Brown, Black, Skin color, Age, Sex etc) on synthesised images usin SOTA GAN models.
  • Bias in Neural Networks: Developed code to study the disparities between ethnic groups.
  • GAN Models: Implemented various GAN models (STAR, CYCLE,BERT, CLIP, VANILLA, QAGAN, FFGAN, etc) for Quality enhancement, attention and various image synthesis applications
  • Multiple Dataset Integration: Collected and annotated of over 400,00 sketches, collated from CelebA, Fair-Face and LFW datasets to implement image synthesis GAN models.
  • Adversarial Attack: Worked on an adversarial framework to compromise classification of neural networks applicable in self driving cars and object detection
  • Image Quality Enhancement: Improved the perceptual quality of images using Image quality assement statistics for both Full reference, Supervised and Unsupervised cases
  • Robotics: Applied both forward and Inverse kinematics for Gesture Control of a robotic Arm
